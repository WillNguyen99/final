Frequently asked questions

Who is the main character?

Yoichi Isagi is the main protagonist, known for his spatial awareness and adaptability.

What is Blue Lock?

Blue Lock is a Japanese manga and anime series about an intense training program created to develop the world's best football striker.

What is the goal of the Blue Lock program?

The goal is to create one striker who can lead Japan to win the World Cup. All participants compete, and only the best survive.

Who is the main character in Blue Lock?

Yoichi Isagi is the main protagonist, a forward who grows by improving his vision, decision-making, and ego.

Why does Blue Lock emphasize “ego”?

The program believes that a great striker must have strong ego, confidence, and the willingness to take responsibility for scoring goals.

Is Blue Lock realistic?

Blue Lock is inspired by real football concepts, but the matches and training are exaggerated for dramatic and entertainment purposes.

Who are some popular characters in Blue Lock?

Popular characters include Yoichi Isagi, Meguru Bachira, Rin Itoshi, Seishiro Nagi, Shouei Barou, and Hyoma Chigiri.

Is Blue Lock more about teamwork or individual skill?

Blue Lock focuses more on individual skill and personal growth, but teamwork becomes important as the story progresses.

Is Blue Lock suitable for beginners who don’t watch football?

Yes. The story explains football concepts in a simple way, making it enjoyable even for viewers who are new to the sport.

Blue Lock Rules

  • Players compete against each other
  • Elimination after each selection
  • Only one striker can succeed
  • Team play is secondary to scoring
  • Failure means leaving the program
